Privacy Policy

Namaz Vakitleri - Adhan & Prayer Times

Last updated: March 19, 2026

1. General Information

The Adhan & Prayer Times application ("App") respects the privacy of its users. This Privacy Policy explains the information collected, used, and protected by the App regarding personal data.

2. Data Collected

The App collects the following data:

  • Location Data: Your device's GPS location is used to determine prayer times and qibla direction. Location data is processed only on your device and is not sent to servers.
  • Notification Preferences: Your preferences for which prayer times you want to receive notifications are stored locally on your device.
  • Language Preference: Your selected app language is stored locally on your device.

3. Use of Data

Collected data is used only for the following purposes:

  • Accurate calculation of prayer times
  • Determining the qibla direction
  • Sending notifications
  • Improving user experience

4. Third-Party Sharing

Your personal data is not shared with third parties. The App may use Google AdMob for advertising services; in this case, Google's privacy policy applies.

5. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Standard security measures are applied to data stored on your device.

6. KVKK and GDPR Compliance

The App operates in compliance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK) No. 6698 and the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You may request the deletion or correction of your personal data.

7. Contact

For questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at burakkocyigit78@gmail.com.
